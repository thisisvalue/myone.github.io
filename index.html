Papa's Hot Doggeria

Papa's Hot Doggeria is the seventh part in the Papa Louie game series where you have to make delicious hot dogs for baseball fans that are coming to Griller Stadium!

It's opening day of baseball season and people are coming to the stadium waiting for thrilling matches. Tickets are sold out. You have to take the role of Taylor or Peggy or a custom worker at Papa's Hot Doggeria and making the best hot dogs for the customers. Take orders, grill up hot dogs, and top them with different condiments to make all of the rowdy fans happy and cheer on your favorite team.

Later in the game, you can unlock new drinks, sauces, popcorns, and flavors. The game also features the Sticker Section. You can receive stickers by completing various tasks.

Game Features:

  • Different holidays to unlock
  • Sticker section
  • Various ingredients to unlock
  • Exciting mini-games
